Registration & Admissions

At Harvard Extension School, we offer multiple paths to enrollment. Whether you want to fill a knowledge gap in a single course or gain a set of new skills through a certificate or degree, you’ll find an accessible path to meet your goals.

Explore our paths of study below. All figures are based on 2019–20 tuition rates.

Our courses are open—no application required to enroll. All it takes is three simple steps.

  1. Choose your course.
  2. Create or update your online account.
  3. Register in one of our enrollment periods. We offer fall, January, spring, and summer classes. See how to register

Applications are not required for our graduate certificates. After you have decided which graduate certificate you want to pursue, simply:

  1. Select your first course with the certificate course search.
  2. Create or update your online account.
  3. Register for graduate credit during one of our enrollment periods.

Learn more about pursuing a certificate and explore our graduate certificates.

Getting Started on a Degree

Our degree programs have an admissions process.

For most of our degrees, your admissions journey starts in the classroom. Complete two or three degree courses with a B or higher to qualify for admission.

To begin your admissions path:

  1. Review the admission and degree requirements for the program that interests you.
  2. Connect with our enrollment team (complete our inquiry form).
  3. Use the degree course search for your program to find the two or three degree courses needed for admission.
  4. Register for your first course either for undergraduate credit, if you are interested in a bachelor's degree, or graduate credit, if you are interested in a master's degree. Note that courses taken prior to admission are not aid eligible.

To view degree requirements, refer to the undergraduate degree program or your graduate degree program field of interest.  

Pursue Premedical Studies

To prepare for medical or dental school, you may fill in gaps from your prior undergraduate education by simply registering for a few medical science courses. Or, you may apply to the Premedical Program to complete all the pre-med coursework, secure advising, and apply for sponsorship.

  1. Explore courses in medical sciences.
  2. Learn about application to Premedical Program.
  3. Register for a course for undergraduate credit or apply to the program.
